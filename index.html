Hey There Here Are The Game Rules :

1-The Game Has 2 Players, Taking Turns To Play.

2-In Each Turn, A Player Rolls A Dice As Many Times As He Whishes. Each Result Gets Added To His Current Score.

3-If The Player Rolls And Gets 1, Their Current Score Becomes 0 And The Second Player's Turn Starts.

4-If A Player Choses To Hold Their Current Score Is Added To Their Final Score And The Next Players Turn Starts.

5-The First Player To Reach Final Score Of 100 Wins!
